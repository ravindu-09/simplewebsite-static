About Us

Your trusted grocery store for fresh and quality products.

Welcome to Rovio Super Market, your one-stop shop for all your daily grocery needs! Located in Wattala, we are committed to providing fresh vegetables, fruits, dairy products, bakery items, and household essentials at the best prices.

  • Friendly customer service
